This website is stored at the University of Granada servers.This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What data do we collect?

If you create an account, we'll collect the following personal data:

  • Your nickname
  • Your mail

In addition, we also collect a password to keep secured your account from unwanted access an we store all your responses in the Train section.

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide us with most all the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you create an account. If you don't register in our site, you will be anonymous for us. Except for your nickname, the rest of your data is encrypted in our database.

How will we use your data?

We need your personal data to create an account for you and manage your training process. In addition, we will generate statistics for you, based in the data you provide. We don't share your data with third parties.

What are your data protection rights?

  • You have the right to request copies of your personal data. But take into consideration that your data is stored encrypted so is not human-readable.
  • You have the right to request us to correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • You have the right to request to erase your personal data.
  • You have the right to request us to restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • You have the right to request us to restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • You have the right to object to processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • You have the right to request to transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
